1. Why Private Equity?

Private equity (PE) means investing in privately held companies—or buyouts of public firms—to drive operational improvements and unlock outsized returns over the long term.

  • Top-quartile net IRR: 20–25%+ over the past decade
  • Average global PE IRR: ~14% vs. ~10% annualized S&P 500 returns
  • 2000–2020 cumulative return: 4.2× for PE vs. 2.5× for the S&P 500

2. Historically Off-Limits

Despite those stellar returns, PE has been gated behind:

  • High minimums: Often $250K+ per fund
  • Accredited-only access: Income & net-worth tests
  • Long lock-ups: 7–10 year capital commitments
  • Complex tax reporting: K-1s, opaque valuations

Everyday investors simply haven’t had a seat at the table—until now.
